The Real-ESRGAN GUI application, launcher, scripts, and project documentation
are licensed under this project's MIT License. The installer shows that license
separately before this notice page.

This page lists third-party software included in, embedded in, or used to
produce the installed application and installer. These summaries are provided
to identify the components and explain how this distribution preserves their
required copyright notices, license conditions, and disclaimers.

For components installed with the application, the complete license texts,
copyright notices, conditions, and disclaimers are bundled with the installed
application. To view them after installation:

1. Open Real-ESRGAN GUI from the Start menu or desktop shortcut.
2. Click About. In Chinese UI, click "关于".
